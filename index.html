GROOM

Matthew Joseph Ehlers

I was born and raised in Niagara, a small town in Northeast Wisconsin. We moved to Wisconsin Rapids in 2008 and I attended the public school there. It wasn't until high school that I formally met Marie. However, our first picture together, which I still have in my room today, is us at confirmation. We officially met through mutual friends at the best place to meet anyone: McDonalds. When I first laid eyes on Marie, she was a sweet ball of energy. I may not have said anything to her on our first date, but I definitely was amazed by her. After warming up to each other, we started to actually talk and hit it off. The rest of our high school years were filled with exploring every park in central Wisconsin, judging which towns had the best playgrounds, and late nights watching movies. After deciding to run off to college together, we continued exploring with our adventurous spirits in Eau Claire. After making the decision to propose to Marie, I decided the best time to do it would be during subzero temperatures near a lake in Madison on New Years Eve. We are looking forward to honeymooning in the Dominican Republic after celebrating our marriage with our friends and family!
